About Wiko Digital

I am an architectural designer based in Birmingham, and own a visualization business cooperating with top 3D visualizers in China. My cooperators are elites in the digital graphic production industry. Together we provide high quality service in architectural and interior rendering, animation, digital modeling and holographic representation.

How We Work

At Wiko Digital, we communicate clearly with our clients to understand their needs. We create a draft design using Sketchup/Revit models, .dwg or .pdf files, and incorporate lighting and atmosphere in the second draft based on their feedback. After several rounds of discussions, we provide a high-resolution image that meets their satisfaction.

Delivery Time

A rendering usually takes about 5-7 days to deliver with regular communications. It might take a little longer if the client requires more rounds of drafts at no extra cost.
